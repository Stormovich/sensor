Introduction

Welcome to the thrilling world of Ball Blaster, an action-packed arcade experience where quick reflexes and sharp aim are your keys to victory! In this fast-paced game, players must blast through waves of bouncing balls that split and multiply with each hit. Dodge, shoot, and survive as you climb through challenging levels, unlock powerful upgrades, and test your skills against increasingly chaotic obstacles. Designed for players of all ages, Ball Blaster combines classic gameplay with modern excitement, delivering endless fun and a serious adrenaline rush. Get ready to blast your way to the top and become the ultimate Ball Blaster champion!

Installation

  1. Download the latest release
  2. Extract the files to your desired location.
  3. Open the source/index.html file in a localserver.
  4. if you have further issues to deploy the game on your liveserver, please contact with me shohan4556@gmail.com

Game Rules

  1. Objective: explode all the balls in the grid by dragging the ship.

  2. Scoring: Players rack up points based on their performance in each roundâ€”factors include the number of balls destroyed, combos achieved, and time survived. Bonus points are awarded for precision shots, clearing levels quickly, or collecting special power-ups. The fewer lives you lose, the higher your score climbs!.

  3. Levels and Difficulty: features a series of progressively challenging levels. As you advance, balls move faster, split into more fragments, and come with new patterns and behaviors.

Game Controls

drag the ships with mouse or touch controls to shoot the balls. The ship will automatically fire at the balls when they are in range.

Customize Ships

modify the source/images/assets.png file to change the ship's appearance.

modify the source/images/assets2.png file to change the game enviroment appearance.

Branding & Logo

To customize the branding of the game, you can replace the logo and other images in the source/images directory. You can also update the game title, description, and other text elements by modifying the gameState.js file.
